Browser Tracking Prevention Notice

If search is not working, your browser's tracking prevention may be blocking requests.

How to fix:
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Uncheck "Prevent cross-site tracking" for localhost
  • Firefox: Click shield icon in address bar → Turn off Enhanced Tracking Protection for this site
  • Brave: Click shield icon → Advanced controls → Disable for this site
  • Chrome/Edge: Should work without changes
